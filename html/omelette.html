Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan.
  3. Pour eggs and cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings when slightly runny.
  5. Fold and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve hot.
